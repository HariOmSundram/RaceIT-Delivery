About us

RaceIT Delivery is a pioneering provider of hyperlocal logistics solutions committed to empowering seamless and sustainable last-mile deliveries for businesses. With a mission to redefine the standards of speed, reliability, and affordability in logistics, RaceIT Delivery offers tailored delivery services across diverse sectors, including food, grocery, e-commerce, and pharmaceuticals. Leveraging advanced technology, a dedicated team, and a commitment to sustainability, RaceIT Delivery ensures exceptional delivery success rates, proactive support, and flexible solutions that exceed customer expectations and drive operational efficiency.

Why Choose Us

Rigorous training: Our delivery personnel undergo extensive training to ensure they handle orders accurately and efficiently, resulting in a high success rate.

Advanced technology: We leverage cutting-edge technology for route optimization and real-time tracking, minimizing the chances of delivery errors and delays.

Dedicated team: Our team leads and cluster managers are available round-the-clock to provide support and address any issues or concerns that may arise.

Customized solutions: We offer personalized solutions tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of each client, ensuring maximum flexibility and satisfaction.

Electric vehicles (EVs): We operate a fleet of electric vehicles (EVs) to reduce carbon emissions and minimize our environmental impact.

Eco-friendly packaging: We use eco-friendly packaging materials to minimize waste and promote sustainability throughout the delivery process.

Green initiatives: We actively participate in green initiatives and invest in eco-friendly practices to contribute to a cleaner and greener environment for future generations.

Modern vehicles: Our delivery fleet consists of modern vehicles equipped with GPS tracking and other advanced features, allowing for efficient navigation and timely deliveries.

Scalable operations: We have the capability to scale our operations based on demand fluctuations, ensuring that we can handle deliveries of all sizes and complexities effectively.
